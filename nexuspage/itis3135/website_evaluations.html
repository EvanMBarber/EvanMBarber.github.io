Arngren.net

Arngren.net

Arngren.net is a great example of poor web design. The site overwhelms users with clutter, inconsistent formatting, and a lack of clear navigation, making it difficult to find information. It almost resembles a teenagers room.

  • Contrast: Poor text/background choices reduce readability. Colors consist of mainly defualts with maximum saturation.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent styles make the page look chaotic. There are multiple font sizes in places where they don't need to be.
  • Alignment: Elements are scattered with little visual organization. There is almost no alignment at all.
  • Proximity: Unrelated content is crammed together and even overlapping.
