About us

For over 15 years, Enraged Barracuda has proudly supplied customers with top-quality diving gear.
From marine scientists and archaeologists to weekend hobbyists, we’ve equipped divers of every skill level with the tools they need to explore the ocean safely and confidently.

Our store carries everything from durable wetsuits and precision-engineered masks to professional-grade oxygen tanks and underwater cameras.
Whether you’re conducting important research, capturing stunning marine life photography, or simply enjoying the thrill of recreational diving, we’ve got the gear to keep you prepared.

At Enraged Barracuda, we don’t just sell equipment—we’re passionate about the diving lifestyle.
Our team is made up of seasoned divers who understand the sea and are always ready to provide expert guidance, gear recommendations, and maintenance tips.

No matter if you’re diving into shallow reefs, braving the open ocean, or exploring sunken wrecks, we’re here to make sure you’re fully equipped for your next adventure.
